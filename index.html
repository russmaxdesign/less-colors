Variables

@base @highlight @lowlight

Darken

Decrease the lightness of a color in the HSL color space by an absolute amount.

@base darken 5% darken 10% darken 15% darken 20%

Lighten

Increase the lightness of a color in the HSL color space by an absolute amount.

@base lighten 5% lighten 10% lighten 15% lighten 20%

Mix

Mix two colors together in variable proportion. Opacity is included in the calculations.

@base 10% 25% 50% 75% 90% @highlight

Saturate

Increase the saturation of a color in the HSL color space by an absolute amount.

@lowlight saturate 25% saturate 50% saturate 75% saturate 100%

Desaturate

Decrease the saturation of a color in the HSL color space by an absolute amount.

@highlight desaturate 25% desaturate 50% desaturate 75% desaturate 100%

Spin

Rotate the hue angle of a color in either direction.

@base spin 90deg spin 180deg spin 270deg

Fade

Set the absolute transparency of a color. Can be applied to colors whether they already have an opacity value or not.

@base 60% 70% 50% 40% 30% 20% 10%

Fade-in

Decrease the transparency (or increase the opacity) of a color, making it more opaque.

10% 20% 30% 40% 50% 60% 70% @base

Fade-out

Decrease the transparency (or increase the opacity) of a color, making it more opaque.

@base 10% 20% 30% 40% 50% 60% 70%

Greyscale

Remove all saturation from a color in the HSL color space; the same as calling desaturate(@color, 100%).

@base High Low
